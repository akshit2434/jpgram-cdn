Uniting for health: tackling the opioid crisis at Joust MUN, where compassion meets action!” 💊🌍
Parola presents WHO at JOUST’24: 
Date: 16-17 November 2024
Venue: Jaypee Institute of information technology, Noida
The World Health Organization (WHO) at Joust MUN is set to tackle the opioid epidemic, a crisis claiming 500,000 lives annually and impacting communities worldwide. Delegates will collaborate on innovative strategies for prevention, treatment, and harm reduction, prioritizing compassion and evidence-based policies. Join us as we develop actionable solutions to combat addiction and promote accessible care, empowering change that resonates beyond the conference! 🌍
